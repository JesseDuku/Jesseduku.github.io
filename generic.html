Projects

A collection of research and engineering work spanning machine learning, computer vision, and water resources.

Plastic Waste Detection with Computer Vision

A YOLOv8 model built during a REVA University data competition to detect plastic waste in rivers — applying computer vision to environmental sustainability and cleaner waterways.

Predicting Punching Shear Failure in Concrete Slabs

Undergraduate thesis: a Support Vector Machine regression model for predicting punching shear in flat concrete slabs, bridging structural engineering and machine learning.

Water Quality Classification

A data-driven study on water potability covering exploratory analysis, preprocessing, and predictive modeling to support safer and more reliable water sources.

Waste Segregation with Computer Vision

A YOLOv8 classifier trained across plastic, glass, paper, and general trash for real-time waste classification with high precision and speed.
