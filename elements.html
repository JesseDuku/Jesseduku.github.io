My goal is to pursue impactful research elucidating the interconnected dynamics of climate, hydrology, and ecology. I am particularly passionate about topics such as drought analysis, flood modeling, coastal hydraulics, and sustainable water resource management. I believe my background in data-driven methods and environmental systems equips me well to pursue graduate studies and an academic career in a field like hydrology or water resources engineering.

Overall, I am committed to conducting solution-driven work that advances scientific understanding and informs policies for resilience and sustainability.

Currently, my research focuses on applying A.I and Remote Sensing to solve problems in the field of hydrosystems engineering, water quality modeling and building Computer Vision algorithms.
